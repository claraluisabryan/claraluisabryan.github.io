My name is Clara Luisa Bryan. I am from Chicago and I am a class of 2021 senior at Wesleyan University studying Computer Science, Digital Computational Media, and Social, Cultural, Critical Theory. I specialize in being able to combine the technical, design, and creative aspects of projects.

I am a learning programmer, animator, graphic designer, music synthesizer and performer, and filmmaker.

I have a working knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Premiere, After Effects, and Illustrator, Python, Java, HTML, CSS, JS, Unity, Rhino and Grasshopper, Ableton, and Max for Live. I love to learn new skills and collaborate with others.

For fun, I enjoy designing and laser cutting plexiglass jewelry, playing music with friends, biking, and spending time exploring outside with friends.
